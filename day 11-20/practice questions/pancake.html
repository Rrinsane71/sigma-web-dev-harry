Delicious Pancake Recipe

These pancakes are fluffy and easy to make, perfect for a weekend breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, sugar).
  2. Add the wet ingredients (milk, eggs) and stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a griddle over medium heat, then pour batter to form pancakes.
  4. Cook each side for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. Serve with syrup and butter.

Enjoy your Pancakes!
